Saga Idol Project

Franchouchou (フランシュシュ, Furanshushu), formerly known as Death Musume or Green Face is a japanese idol group whose members aim is to bring back their home prefecture of Saga its long lost popularity in media and pop culture.

Saga Prefecture

The land we fight for

Saga Prefecture (佐賀県, Saga-ken), is a prefecture on Kyushu Island and one of the most beautiful areas in all of Japan, known for its history, culture, natural wonders and some of the best traditional food our country has to offer. This is the land we fight to bring back on the map of international tourism, not only Japan. All our memories, the beautiful places and people we grew up with is what we want you, our fans to experience as we once did.
